02 — The Meridian AI Clinical Engine

All your data.
A million studies.
One top priority.

Your genome, your blood, your wearables, your history — together that's roughly 300,000 tokens of information. A physician's working memory holds about 150.

The Engine convenes four frontier AI models in a structured medical-board review of your full picture — cross-referenced against the BioMCP medical-knowledge graph and the OpenEvidence research index — then hands the synthesis to a Meridian MD. The AI does what AI is good at: hold everything at once. Your physician does what physicians are good at: judgment, gut check, and a real conversation with someone who knows your name.

03 — Your Meridian MD

A real physician.
Holding your picture.

The Engine does the holding-everything-at-once part. Your Meridian MD does the part you can't automate — the judgment, the gut check, the call you actually want to take.

When something matters, you'll get a voice message from your physician — not a notification, not a chatbot summary. They'll explain what they're seeing, why it matters for you specifically, and the one thing to do this week. If you have a question, message them. If you need a visit, book one. They already have your full picture.
