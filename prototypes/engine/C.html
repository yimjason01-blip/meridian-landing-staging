02 — The Meridian Clinical Engine

All your data. A million studies. One top priority.

Most "AI in healthcare" is one model giving one answer. The Engine convenes four frontier models in a structured medical-board review of your full picture — like a tumor board, but for everything that could shape your trajectory.

The board convenes

Four frontier models, each looking at your full picture.

Each model sees the same case — your genome, your labs, your wearables, your history. Each works the problem independently, cross-referenced against the BioMCP medical-knowledge graph and the OpenEvidence research index. Independence is the point — agreement after independent review is signal.

They deliberate

They disagree, on purpose.

Where models concur, the finding strengthens. Where they conflict, the conflict gets surfaced — not buried. A single model is a single point of view. Four in structured debate is closer to the way real medicine works on a hard case.

The handoff

The output goes to a person who knows your name.

The Engine never decides. It synthesizes — and hands a single prioritized finding, with the evidence and dissent attached, to your Meridian MD. They make the call. They send the voice message. The AI did what AI is good at. The human did what humans are good at.
