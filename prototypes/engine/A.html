02 — The Meridian Clinical Engine

All your data. A million studies. One top priority.

Six streams enter the engine. Your genome. Your labs. Your wearables. Your history. Your imaging. Sleep and HRV. None of them mean anything alone.

The streams

Six data sources, one continuous picture.

Most clinics see one slice — last week's blood draw, yesterday's blood pressure. The Engine reads all of it, all the time. The streams stay live; the picture stays current.

The chamber

300,000 tokens, compressed to a question.

Inside the chamber, four frontier models conduct a structured medical-board review of your full picture, cross-referenced against the BioMCP medical-knowledge graph and the OpenEvidence research index. A physician's working memory holds about 150 tokens. The chamber holds everything.

The signal

One thing that actually matters for you.

The Engine doesn't generate a 40-page report. It hands a Meridian MD a single prioritized finding with the evidence behind it. Your physician makes the call, sends you a voice message, and tells you the one thing to do this week.
