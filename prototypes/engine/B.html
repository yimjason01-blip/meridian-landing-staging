02 — The Meridian Clinical Engine

All your data. A million studies. One top priority.

Three layers of machinery. Each one doing the part of medicine no human can do alone, then handing the result to the next layer. Like a powertrain — each component matters, the whole is what makes it move.

Layer 1 · Risk Models

Five domain models, running continuously.

Cardiovascular. Metabolic. Oncologic. Neurologic. Renal. Each model takes your full data picture — genome, labs, wearables, history — and computes one number: how is this trajectory bending, and how fast.

Layer 2 · Attention

Ranked, so you don't have to be.

The Attention Layer takes outputs from all five risk models and ranks them by what's actually changeable, today, with the highest-leverage intervention. Most prevention products give you a 40-page report. We give you the priority queue.

Layer 3 · Action

One thing to do this week.

The Action Layer translates the top priority into a concrete plan, attaches the evidence, and hands it to a Meridian MD. They review, customize for your life, and send you a voice message. Not a 40-page report. The next thing to do.
