Section 02 — Clinical Engine

Three executions of the same idea

Same headline. Same core claim — all your data, a million studies, one top priority. Three different metaphors for what makes the engine real and trustworthy.

Option A · The Sensor

Streams flow into a chamber

A precision instrument quietly reading your life.

Six data streams (genome, labs, wearables, sleep/HRV, history, imaging) flow downward into a glowing chamber. The chamber compresses 300,000 tokens into one prioritized signal that exits as a beam to the bottom card.

 Option B · The Powertrain

Three planes stacked in 3D

Precision engineering — you can see the cogs.

Exploded-view machine. Three planes — Risk Models, Attention Layer, Action Layer — float in 3D space. Each scroll beat illuminates one plane and surfaces its function with a real artifact (5 domain tiles, ranked priority list, action card).

Reference → Rivian quad-motor · "engineered transparency"
 Option C · The Boardroom

Four models deliberating around a case

Not a chatbot — a structured medical board.

A round table with a synthetic patient case in the center. Four frontier-model voices around the perimeter speak in turn — independent review, then deliberation, then consensus handed to a human MD. Most novel-mechanism feel.
